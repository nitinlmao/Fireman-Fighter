Understanding the Types of Fire Extinguishers

Fire extinguishers come in various types, each designed to combat specific types of fires. The most common types include water, foam, powder, and CO2 extinguishers. Water extinguishers are suitable for Class A fires involving solid materials, while foam extinguishers work well on Class A and B fires. Powder extinguishers are effective against Class A, B, and C fires, while CO2 extinguishers are suitable for electrical fires (Class E). Understanding these differences is essential for choosing the right extinguisher for specific fire risks.
